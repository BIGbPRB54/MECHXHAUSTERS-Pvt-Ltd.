FALCON

Engineered for passionate motorsport enthusiasts, the Falcon delivers responsive performance, precise control, and a lightweight platform — all upgradeable to Phoenix spec.

Engine: Single-Cylinder, Liquid-Cooled
Horsepower: 43 HP
Torque: 35 Nm
Top Speed: 125 km/h
Transmission: 6-speed Manual

Key Features:

  • 10” Monotone LCD Cluster with essential performance telemetry
  • Lightweight alloy wheels for agile handling and reduced unsprung mass
  • Sport-calibrated suspension tuned for daily and track use
  • Open Differential for raw mechanical feedback and driver feel
  • Reinforced safety cell with basic driver protection ergonomics
  • Upgradeable architecture to Phoenix after 2 years
Inquire About Falcon

PHOENIX

A track-enhanced upgrade over the Falcon, the Phoenix is built for professionals seeking performance, telemetry, and aerodynamic advantage powered by an ECU-tuned engine.

Engine: ECU-Tuned Single-Cylinder
Horsepower: ~47+ HP
Torque: 38 Nm
Top Speed: 150 km/h
Transmission: 6-speed Manual

Key Innovations:

  • ECU-Mapped for responsive track dynamics and throttle control
  • AI-Enhanced Vehicle Stability and Traction Control Systems
  • Data Logging Suite with Mobile App Access
  • Adjustable Aero Kit for high-speed cornering performance
  • Fully Digital Dashboard with Race Telemetry Mode
  • Convertible to different racing configurations with modular chassis mounts
